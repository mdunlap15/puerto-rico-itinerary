Old San Juan · Feb 28 – Mar 3
A restored 350-year-old Carmelite convent and Small Luxury Hotels of the World member. Rooftop plunge pool and jacuzzi overlooking San Juan Bay, two on-site restaurants, nightly wine & cheese reception, and included beach privileges at their Beach Club on Isla Verde Beach. Steps from the Cathedral, El Morro, and dozens of restaurants.
100 Calle del Cristo +1 787-723-9020 elconvento.com

Blue Horizon Boutique Resort
Vieques Island · Mar 3 – Mar 6
Adults-only oceanfront resort perched on a clifftop overlooking the Caribbean, with 50 acres of beachfront. Mediterranean-style main house, infinity pool, private beach access, and the Carambola restaurant with legendary brunch. Only 12 rooms — intimate and secluded. Rent a Jeep and explore the island's empty beaches.

Day by Day
The Itinerary
01
Saturday, February 28
Arrival & First Night
El Convento
Afternoon

Noon flight from NYC, land ~3pm. Uber to Old San Juan (~25 min). Check into Hotel El Convento and settle in.

Sunset

Walk the Paseo de la Princesa — a gorgeous waterfront promenade along the old city walls. Grab a piragua (Puerto Rican shaved ice) from a street vendor.

Dinner

Marmalade (upscale Caribbean tasting menu), Pirilo Pizza (craft cocktails & Neapolitan pizza), or El Picoteo at El Convento for tapas and wine.

02
Sunday, March 1
Old San Juan Exploration + La Placita
El Convento
11:30am — Brunch

Kaffe Haus (311 Calle de Tetuán) — live jazz, Puerto Rican Eggs Benedict, generous portions. Or Chocobar Cortés (210 Calle San Francisco) for chocolate French toast and mallorcas.

12:45pm — Walk to El Morro

Leave Kaffe Haus and walk uphill through the old city past the cemetery lawn. ~20 minutes on foot.

1:05pm — Castillo San Felipe del Morro

El Morro — $10/person, covers both forts. Six levels of ramparts, the iconic lighthouse, cannon placements, and sweeping ocean views. Allow about 70 minutes.

2:15pm — Walk to San Cristóbal

Walk east along the old city walls with great views. ~20 minutes.

2:35pm — Castillo San Cristóbal

San Cristóbal — same ticket. The tunnels here are the real highlight. Allow about 55 minutes.

3:30pm — Walk to Ferry

Downhill walk south to Pier 2 ferry terminal. ~15 minutes.

4:00pm — Ferry to Cataño

Catch the Cataño ferry (runs every 30 min, ~15 min crossing, under $1). Short walk to Casa Bacardí on arrival.

4:30pm — Casa Bacardí

Rum tasting or mixology class (~$50–70/person, 60–90 min). Ferry back to Old San Juan around 6pm.

~8:00pm — La Placita

Uber to La Placita de Santurce — legendary Sunday night salsa, dancing in the streets, cheap drinks, incredible local energy.

☆ Don't miss La Placita. Sunday night is the best night. It's where the locals go and one of the best nightlife experiences in the Caribbean.

03
Monday, March 2
Beach & Pool Day
El Convento
All Day

Sleep in and recover from La Placita. Head to Vivo Beach Club in Isla Verde (~20 min Uber) or use El Convento's included Isla Verde Beach Club. Arrive around 11am, grab poolside loungers, and alternate between the pool and ocean all day. Order food and drinks from your chair.

Dinner

Last night in Old San Juan — try somewhere you haven't been. Raices for traditional Puerto Rican or La Madre for upscale Mexican with great cocktails.

04
Tuesday, March 3
El Yunque → Ferry → Vieques → Bio Bay
Blue Horizon
Morning

Check out of El Convento early. Uber/drive east to El Yunque National Forest (~1 hr) — the only tropical rainforest in the US. Hike to natural waterslides and swimming holes. Bring water shoes and a change of clothes.

Lunch

Luquillo Beach Kiosks — dozens of open-air food stalls right on the beach serving mofongo, empanadillas, and fresh seafood.

Afternoon

Continue to Ceiba Ferry Terminal (~20 min from Luquillo). Take the ~30 min ferry to Vieques. Pick up your rental Jeep at the dock. Drive to Blue Horizon Boutique Resort and check in.

Evening

Mosquito Bay bioluminescent kayak tour — the world's brightest bio bay. Microscopic organisms light up the water as you paddle through the darkness.

☆ The big adventure day. Plan ferry times carefully — check puertoricoferry.com for the schedule. Book the bio bay tour in advance.

05
Wednesday, March 4
Vieques Beach Day #1
Blue Horizon
All Day

Full day exploring Vieques by Jeep. Hit La Chiva (Blue Beach), Sun Bay, and Playa Caracas — some of the most pristine, uncrowded beaches in the Caribbean. Pack snorkeling gear, a cooler, and reef-safe sunscreen.

Afternoon

Back to Blue Horizon's infinity pool for sunset cocktails overlooking the Caribbean.

Dinner

Carambola at the hotel (their brunch and seafood mofongo are legendary), or drive into Esperanza for local spots along the malecón.

06
Thursday, March 5
Vieques Beach Day #2
Blue Horizon
Morning

The bonus day. Book a horseback ride on the beach or a massage at Blue Horizon (cliff-side or poolside).

All Day

Explore beaches you missed — Playa Negra (black sand beach), Media Luna, or snorkel at a remote cove. Visit the old Navy bunkers and sugar mill ruins scattered around the island.

Evening

Sunset cocktails at the hotel. Last dinner on the island — try somewhere in Esperanza you haven't been yet.

07
Friday, March 6
Morning & Departure
Blue Horizon
Morning

Leisurely morning at the resort. Breakfast at Carambola, one last dip in the infinity pool. Check out and return the Jeep.

Midday

Ferry back to Ceiba. Drive/Uber to SJU airport (~1.5 hours). Afternoon flight home.

☆ Allow plenty of time. Budget ~2.5 hours total from Vieques to the SJU gate. Check ferry schedules the night before.
